
# 📊 Vrinda Store Sales Analysis

## 🔍 Project Overview

This project analyzes retail sales data from Vrinda Store using Microsoft Excel. The objective was to extract meaningful business insights related to customer behavior, regional performance, and sales trends to support data-driven decision-making.

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies

* Microsoft Excel
* Pivot Tables & Pivot Charts
* Data Cleaning & Transformation
* Dashboard Design

---

## 📈 Business Problems Addressed

* Identifying peak sales months and order trends
* Understanding customer demographics (gender & age)
* Analyzing regional sales performance
* Evaluating order delivery status
* Determining top-performing sales channels

---

## 📊 Key Insights

* Women contribute approximately **64%** of total sales
* Maharashtra, Karnataka, and Uttar Pradesh are the **top revenue-generating states**
* Amazon (~35%), Flipkart (~22%), and Myntra (~23%) are the **major sales channels**
* Around **92% of orders are successfully delivered**
* Adult women segment contributes the highest share in sales
